General Rules for a "Fun Battle"

  • Lobby Targets: Requires enough tickets to fill 18 players per faction (2-factions) or 12 players per faction (3-factions / Encounter).
  • The 50% Core Rule: At least half of the required player base per faction must be explicitly supplied by Infantry or Paratrooper tickets.
  • Class Ticket Thresholds: A single player allocation requires 12 Infantry/Paratrooper* tickets, or 10 Tank/Plane/Recon tickets.
  • *Note: Airborne Paratrooper units require 12 spawns and 4 paraplanes per ticket build.
